Klarna is now one of Europe's largest banks and provides payment solutions for as many as 90 million consumers, across more than 200,000 merchants in 19 countries. In addition, Klarna acquired Sofort in 2013, which means that this direct payment method is also included in the Klarna offering.
iDeal:
iDEAL is a Dutch standard method for making internet payments and is the property of Currence.

Bancontact is a payment scheme, known until 2016 as Bancontact/Mister Cash, which is the Belgian market leader in electronic payments. As a payment scheme, it is responsible for checking, calculating and processing electronic payments. These payments can be made via debit cards.
